PohonLink Philosophy

The philosophy of this link tree comes from the words "tree" and "link" which means a tree that can be linked or can be branched with 1 tree with 1 other tree or easily connected.

Why did we choose this name for our website?

Because for us, the pohon link symbolizes a symbol of strength that symbolizes the unity between the tree and its branches so it is not easily damaged / broken. In the implementation of our website as well, we want to provide a strong and reliable website can contain many links so that it can facilitate the input process from users when creating their respective links.

What does the green-yellow color mean on our website?

Green which means symbolizing life and yellow is a color that is peaceful or as the color of happiness combined, it will mix life and happiness so that it makes our website a living website.
